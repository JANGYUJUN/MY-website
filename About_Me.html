About Me

I design intuitive, human-centered products by combining research-driven UX strategy with clear visual communication.

Before product design, I spent years illustrating for media & content teams, collaborating closely with cross-functional partners. That experience sharpened my ability to communicate complex ideas visually and build purposeful, delightful systems.

Now, I bring that same strategic eye to crafting digital experiences that are not just functional, but meaningful. I specialize in research synthesis, UX flows, and information architecture grounded in a deep understanding of human behavior.
